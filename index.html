The Generative AI for Work & Research program is an 8-week live cohort course focused on responsible no-code AI tools for task automation, research productivity, communication, and data analysis.

MODULE 1

Websites, Documents & Presentations

Learning how large language models work, creating structured research documents, building websites, and generating AI-assisted scientific presentations.

MODULE 2

AI-Automated Data Analysis

Using AI tools for dataset processing, automated reporting, visualization, dashboards, and ethical data analysis workflows.

MODULE 3

Research & Text Analysis

Exploring literature reviews, text classification, sentiment analysis, summarization systems, and knowledge synthesis with AI.

MODULE 4

Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Building AI chatbots, retrieval systems, and knowledge assistants for research and productivity support.

AI for Task Automation
